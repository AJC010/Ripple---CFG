What's going on?

  • Indoor Climbing

    Some days, experiencing the city from a plain old horizontal perspective just isn’t enough. On those occasions, as with most others, London will always provide the answer: and the answer is strapping yourself in and climbing up a pretend mountain, obviously.

    7pm, 11th April

  • Blind Group Date

    Whether it’s young professionals looking to meet other singles or for a friendly night out, this event aims to disrupt the bar scene and traditional one-on-one dating with a blind date opportunity, where two groups of friends meet for the first time.

    6pm, 19th April

  • Silent Disco

    Bringing together the holy trinity of silent disco, retro gaming and rooftop raving, this unique night of partying at the Altitude SkyBar is the only event you big kids will want to be getting down to this Spring!

    9pm, 26th April

  • Dinner with Elon Musk

    If you want to change the future, invent a better one. So thinks Elon Musk, the billionaire inventor of electric cars and space rockets and co-founder of the internet payment system PayPal. Spend an unforgettable evening with the man himself this summer.

    7pm, 15th April

  • Kew Garden outing

    Kew Gardens, London’s largest UNESCO World Heritage Site, is the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. It's home to the world’s most diverse collection of living plants and a scientific research centre of international renown.

    3pm, 20th April

  • Sushi Making

    3 hour hands-on workshop covering everything you need to know to make your own sushi! Japanese tea for the thirsty sushi chefs and a big sushi dinner afterwards where you can enjoy your own handmade sushi!

    12pm, 27th April

  • DIY and Craft Projects

    Ever fancied yourself as a crafty little sew-and-sew? If there ever was a time to get your hands dirty (and sticky, and fluffy, and glittery) it's now - From printing to pom poms, firing pots to feminist embroidery. Knitting isn’t just for your Nan. Knitting is cool now.

    4pm, 17th April

  • Midnight running

    A movement born out of a love for our city, and a desire to escape the confines of the gym. It's open to athletes and novices alike, of all ages, nationalities, disciplines and backgrounds, united by a passion for running - with a twist.

    11pm, 20th April

  • Wine Tasting

    Perfect for work events or large groups, The London Wine School is the wine masterclass that comes to you. The host of teachers and experts are armed with an arsenal of grape-y facts, bottles of vino and methods to make the most of your wine.

    5pm, 28th April
